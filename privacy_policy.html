Privacy Policy for Perhaiz

Last Updated: March 01, 2026

Summary

Perhaiz is a dietary assistant application. We collect health-related data (such as dietary restrictions and medical conditions) strictly to provide scanning and recommendation features. We do not sell your personal health data to third parties.

1. Introduction

Welcome to Perhaiz ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience on our mobile application.

This policy explains how we handle your personal information, especially sensitive health data, in compliance with Google Play and Apple App Store policies.

2. Information We Collect

A. Personal Information

  • Account Information: Name, Email address (for authentication via Firebase).
  • Profile Data: Age, Gender, Language preference.

B. Sensitive Health Data

  • Medical Conditions: Diseases or allergies you explicitly add to your profile (e.g., Diabetes, Hypertension, Nut Allergy).
  • Dietary Preferences: Precautions you select (e.g., Low Sugar, Gluten Free).

Purpose: This data is used SOLELY to analyze food products and warn you about potential health risks. It is stored securely on Google Cloud Firestore and your local device.

C. Device Permissions

  • Camera: We request camera access to scan product barcodes. No images are saved to our servers; processing happens on-device or temporarily for OCR analysis.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide personalized food safety analysis.
  • To sync your profile across your devices.
  • To improve app performance and fix bugs.

4. Data Sharing and Third Parties

We do NOT sell your personal data. We use the following trusted third-party services:

  • Google Firebase: For authentication, database hosting, and analytics.
  • OpenAI API: We may send anonymized product text to AI services to generate nutritional information. Your personal health profile is NOT shared with OpenAI; only the product text is analyzed against a set of rules.

5. Data Security

We implement security measures including encryption (SSL) and secure database rules to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

6. Your Rights (Data Deletion)

You have the right to delete your account and all associated data at any time.

How to Delete: Go to Settings > Account > Delete Account within the app. This will permanently remove your profile, health data, and scan history from our servers immediately.

7. Medical Disclaimer

Perhaiz is an information tool, not a medical device. The dietary analysis provided is for reference only and may not be 100% accurate. Always consult a qualified healthcare professional for medical advice and verify product labels manually.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page.
